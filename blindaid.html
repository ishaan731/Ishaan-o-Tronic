Blindaid

A device that helps blind people on the road from obstacles !

This project was made by me for helping blind people guard

their way on the road as many of them encounter accidents

which can lead to severe injuries. A blind person can put

the device on his belt as it fits in any belt perfectly.

An obstacle such as a car or a bus or any other vehicle will be

detected within a range of 5m. If it detects the

obstacle, it will make a buzzing sound so that he

gets to know that an obstacle is in front of him and he

needs to move away.
