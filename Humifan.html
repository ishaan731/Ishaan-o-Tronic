Humi-fan

an energy efficient project....

Humifan is a project made by me that

detects the temperature of the room.

If the temperature of the room is

above 28 degree Celsius, then the fan starts

rotating; and as the temperature rises

the fan speed also increases. It consists of

Arduino Uno, temperature sensor and a

DC motor. It is an energy efficient project
