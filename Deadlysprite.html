Deadly Sprite

A truly amazing game for children !

Hello, friends, this is my new project called

deadly sprite. it mainly uses a button, an LCD board,

and a micro-controller like an Arduino.so when we

press the button .the sprite will jump over the

bricks like this and the sprite will come down

when the bricks are over.as you can see there

is also a score board on the top upper side .

It is not an only exciting game but it also

works on the focus levels of children during

this lockdown !
