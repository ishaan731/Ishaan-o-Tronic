COVID Social Distancer Band

A truly amazing band to protect us from COVID

This is a very cool band called the

COVID Social Distancer Band. It basically

notifies us to keep a distance among

people, as we often forget about it while

talking to someone. It has a sensor that

checks if a person is within 6 feet of distance

or not. If he is within 6ft of distance, the

buzzer inside the watch will beep until the

two people maintain a distance. It is a very useful

band and can help thousands of people to overcome COVID.
