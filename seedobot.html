Seedobot

A robot that can save us from the perils oof deforestation!

The forest fires in india have increased and lead to

lot of deforestation .Robot “Seedobot” has been programed

using Arduino UNO and sensors to identify a spot in the barren

forest at a distance of 1m in a line path and drop a seed, so that human

intervention in forests is zero due to various reasons. Solar

panels are attached to it and would take the natural solar power to

work. I have used a power bank for the prototype to work.

With all the natural conditions required for seed germination- clear rich

nutrient land and direct sunlight falling, we can aim for

afforestation in areas where risk of soil erosion is least.

Even if we aim for 40-50% successful germination, most of

the green cover loss can be recovered- which otherwise without

the human intervention would remain barren.
